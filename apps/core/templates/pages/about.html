Our Story


Several years ago, I stumbled upon a sweet, black kitty named Daphne. She was one of the most popular cats in the neighborhood; she'd see you and slip right through the rod-iron gates to say hello. She was well known among the locals, and she never strayed farm from her humble abode.

It turned out that Daphne was no ordinary kitty: she was cat royalty. One day, she pledged her paw to the Dauphin of Colorado, Prince Eduard III, Duke of the Rockies. The two were to be betrothed, and this caused great alarm in the surrounding regions. No longer would her majesty grace the company of the local citizens.

A notice was posted so that all locals could pay their respects before her long journey to the Rockies. Many came to honor her majesty and bid her a heartfelt farewell.

In her honor, we created this site to bring the joy and memory of all our favorite Next Door Cats.
